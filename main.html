Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is one of the most visited cities in the world. Known as the "City of Light," it is renowned for its art, culture, history, and romantic atmosphere.

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic symbol of Paris offering breathtaking views.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa and thousands of masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A stunning example of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – Famous avenue for shopping, dining, and walking.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A beautiful way to see Paris illuminated at night.
  • Croissants – Flaky and buttery French breakfast essential.
  • Escargot – Traditional French dish made with snails and garlic butter.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes that can be sweet or savory.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate almond meringue cookies with filling.
  • French Cheese – A must-try for food lovers visiting Paris.
